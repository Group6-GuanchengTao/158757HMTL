Adopt a star

    You can use this page to participate in our Adopt-a-Star program and freely name your adopted star. The purpose of this donation campaign is to provide financial support for our exploration and promote human exploration and development in the universe.

    By making your donation, you will not only participate in the magnificent human endeavor to explore the universe, but you will also perpetuate your name, or the name you wish to name, as a memorial to your generous donation. This will be your unique contribution to the journey of interstellar exploration.

    Your generous donation will be an important impetus for mankind to pursue the space dream. Let us join hands and jointly open up a broader future of space exploration.
